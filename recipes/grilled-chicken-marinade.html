Grilled Chicken Marinade

Description

This marinade for grilled chicken is so flavorful and so easy to prep with easy pantry ingredients. It's simply the best chicken marinade for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Spices and Seasoning: Dried parsely flakes,dried basil, dried oregano,garlic powder & ground black pepper

Steps

  1. 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  2. 1/4 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
  3. 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoons dried parsely flakes
  5. 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  6. 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  7. 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  9. 5 skinless, boneless chicken breast thinly sliced
